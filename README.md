### 🔬 Research & Focus

I am an AI Researcher and Engineer focused on the intersection of **representation learning**, **large language models**, and **production-ready agentic systems**. My work spans from probing the internal representations of frontier models to engineering high-reliability, multimodal inference pipelines.

- **Interpretability:** Probing latent concept stability across layers and context windows in frontier LLMs.
- **Multimodal Systems:** Efficient fine-tuning of VLMs and building multi-view reasoning pipelines..
- **Agentic Infrastructure:** Architecting multi-step agent workflows with fallback routing and observability.
- **Evaluation & Serving:** Developing rigorous baselines, regression suites , and optimizing inference.
